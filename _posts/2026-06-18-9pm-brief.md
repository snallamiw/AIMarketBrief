### KEY DRIVERS

**U.S.-Iran Peace Deal (MOU Signed)**

This ended the active blockade and geopolitical risk premium that had pushed Brent to $126/bbl at the peak of the conflict. The Iran relief overshadowed the hawkish shift in today's session; expect this tension to reassert.

This is a structural positive for U.S. chip manufacturing policy and reshoring narrative — likely not fully priced in across the supply chain.
