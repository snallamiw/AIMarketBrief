**US and Iran announce their war is over, with Trump declaring the deal "now complete" and the Strait of Hormuz set to reopen.**

## World News

**US-Iran war ends, Strait of Hormuz to reopen.** President Trump posted that "The Deal with the Islamic Republic of Iran is now complete," with Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Kazem Gharibabadi confirming the agreement will be signed in Switzerland on Friday. The deal lifts the US naval blockade and allows toll-free shipping through the Strait of Hormuz, which has been largely closed since the US and Israel launched their assault on Iran in late February. Trump hinted a broader regional peace agreement could follow. Oil prices fell sharply on the news. Why it matters: this ends a months-long war that disrupted global energy markets; watch for the Friday signing and whether the ceasefire holds through it. Sources: [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/6/14/us-iran-ceasefire-deal-announced-trump-says-strait-of-hormuz-reopening), [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/06/14/world/live-news/iran-war-trump-israel), [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/deal-reached-united-states-iran-war-rcna350039)

**Israel says it is not bound by the US-Iran deal.** Israeli officials stated the agreement does not require Israel to end its fight with Hezbollah or withdraw forces from Lebanon. Why it matters: this keeps a separate front of the conflict open even as the headline US-Iran war winds down, and could complicate the durability of the broader deal. Source: [TheStreet](https://www.thestreet.com/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-updates-june-15-2026)

**Russia launches large-scale attack on Kharkiv and Kyiv.** A second Russian strike on Kharkiv killed five rescuers who were fighting a blaze from an earlier attack; at least 20 people were wounded in Kyiv. Strikes set apartment buildings ablaze and sparked a serious fire at the Kyiv-Pechersk Lavra, one of Ukraine's most significant religious landmarks, damaging the Dormition Cathedral's roof. Why it matters: a sharp escalation in Russian strikes on civilian and cultural sites even as Western attention is focused on the Middle East. Source: [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/06/15/g-s1-128118/russia-ukraine-war)

## US News

**Markets jump on Iran deal news.** Nasdaq futures rose on the announcement that the US-Iran war is ending and the Strait of Hormuz will reopen, with broader relief across equity and oil markets. Why it matters: easing a major geopolitical risk that had been weighing on energy prices and market sentiment. Source: [TheStreet](https://www.thestreet.com/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-updates-june-15-2026)

**Bill Gates testifies in Epstein investigation.** Gates appeared before the House Oversight Committee in a closed-door interview, telling lawmakers that meeting with Jeffrey Epstein was "a grave error in judgment" and that he "never witnessed nor had any indication" Epstein was engaged in ongoing criminal conduct. He said he never visited Epstein's island, ranch, or Florida home. A transcript is expected within days. Why it matters: Gates is among the highest-profile figures interviewed as the committee continues probing the government's handling of the Epstein case. Sources: [ABC News](https://abcnews.com/US/bill-gates-face-questions-house-oversight-panel-relationship/story?id=133685951), [PBS](https://www.pbs.org/newshour/politics/what-to-know-about-bill-gates-relationship-with-jeffrey-epstein-as-he-is-interviewed-in-house-probe)

**Fox Corp to acquire Roku for $22 billion.** Fox will pay $96 cash plus 0.9693 Fox Class A shares per Roku share, valuing Roku at roughly $22 billion in enterprise value. The deal combines Fox's sports, news and entertainment content (including Tubi) with Roku's platform, which reaches over 100 million global streaming households. Existing Fox shareholders would own about 73% of the combined company. Both boards approved unanimously; closing expected in H1 2027. Why it matters: creates the third-largest player in US television by share of viewing time, reshaping the streaming and live-sports landscape. Source: [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-15/fox-to-buy-roku-at-22-billion-value-in-streaming-video-push)

## India News

**Modi holds bilateral talks with Macron, unveils India-France "Innovation Roadmap 2030."** The roadmap establishes a Joint India-France AI Working Group and a Dialogue on Economic Security focused on critical-minerals supply chains, and pushes for faster conclusion of the India-EU FTA. The PM also welcomed the US-Iran understanding on ending the West Asia conflict. Why it matters: deepens India-France strategic and tech ties ahead of a potential India-EU trade deal. Source: [PM India](https://www.pmindia.gov.in/en/news-updates/)

**Indian Army issues new uniform regulations.** The "Army Uniforms-2026 Pamphlet" progressively removes residual colonial-era practices, terminology, and non-essential accoutrements, aligning uniforms with contemporary Indian values. Why it matters: part of a broader push to update military traditions and symbolism.

**Monsoon advances; rain hits Delhi.** Light rain and thunderstorms brought relief to the capital, with the IMD forecasting more showers and gusty winds; Delhi's high is expected near 39°C. The southwest monsoon is set to advance into more of Maharashtra, Karnataka, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, West Bengal, Odisha, Jharkhand, Bihar and South Chhattisgarh over the next 4-5 days, with widespread rainfall expected over Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram and Tripura through June 20. Why it matters: monsoon progress is closely watched for agriculture and water supply nationwide.
